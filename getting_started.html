Welcome to SnapOCR - Text Extraction Guide

Step 1: Capture an Image

Using the Camera app, capture an image of the document or text you want to extract.

Step 2: Upload the Image

Go to the SnapOCR website and click on the "Upload" button. Select the image file from your device and upload it.

Step 3: Process the Image

After uploading the image, SnapOCR will process it to extract the text. This may take a few moments depending on the size of the image.

Step 4: View Extracted Text

Once the text extraction process is complete, you will see the extracted text displayed on the screen. You can copy and use the text as needed.

Step 5: Download Extracted Text

If you want to save the extracted text, you can click on the "Download" button to download it as a text file.

Step 6: Clear and Start Over

If you want to extract text from another image, you can click on the "Clear" button to remove the current image and start the process again.

Step 7: Contact Support

If you encounter any issues or have questions, feel free to contact our support team at support@snapocr.com.
